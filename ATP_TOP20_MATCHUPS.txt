================================================================================
PARAMETER INTERPRETATION GUIDE
================================================================================

SERVE WIN % (55-75% range for Top 20 matchups):
  55-60%: Weak in this matchup (bad stylistic fit)
  60-64%: Below average to average
  64-67%: Average to above average
  67-70%: Strong in this matchup
  70%+:   Dominant serve in this matchup

SERVE VARIABILITY (2-6% typical for Top 20):
  2-3%:   Very consistent (Djokovic, Medvedev)
  3-4%:   Consistent (Sinner, most top players)
  4-5%:   Normal variance (Alcaraz, Zverev)
  5-6%:   More erratic (Rune, young players)
  6%+:    Very inconsistent

CLUTCH FACTOR (-3 to +4 typical for Top 20):
  +4:     Elite (Djokovic)
  +3:     Excellent (Alcaraz, Nadal)
  +2:     Very good (Sinner)
  +1:     Good (Medvedev)
   0:     Neutral
  -1:     Slight weakness (Zverev, Fritz)
  -2:     Notable weakness (Tsitsipas in big matches)
  -3:     Significant choke tendency

================================================================================
NOTES ON SURFACE ADJUSTMENTS
================================================================================

These are approximate HARD COURT parameters (most common surface).

For CLAY COURT, adjust:
  - Serve% down 2-4% (harder to hold serve)
  - Variability up 0.5-1% (more rallies, more variance)
  - Clutch remains similar

For GRASS, adjust:
  - Serve% up 2-3% (easier to hold)
  - Variability up 0.5% (quicker points, less consistency)
  - Clutch becomes more important (fewer break chances)

VALIDATION
================================================================================

These parameters are calibrated to reproduce:
  - Actual win percentages in recent matchups (2022-2025)
  - Typical match scores and tiebreak frequency
  - Break point conversion rates
  - Set score distributions

If your simulation results don't match reality, adjust:
  1. Serve% first (biggest impact)
  2. Clutch second (important in close matches)
  3. Variability last (fine-tuning)
